Cards

All players draw a card upon entering a Dangerous Sector. Depending on what type of card it is, you will make some sort of noise, then either discard it (if it's a noise card) or keep it. (if it's an Item card and the player is a Human)

Vanilla Cards

These cards were ported more or less directly from the physical version of the game with minimal changes. Cards that have been considerably changed from the tabletop version are marked with an asterisk (*) and the exact changes are italicized. In general, any card that doesn't have an immediate effect (like Attack, for example) will now give a Status Effect.

Noise Cards

Noise Cards are immediately discarded upon being drawn, causing the Player to make noise depending on the card.

Red Card

Make noise in the sector you just moved into.

Green Card

Make noise in any sector of your choosing.

White Card

Make no noise.

Item Cards

All players make no noise upon drawing an Item card. Additionally, Humans add the Item to their hand and may use the card at any point after drawing it, including on the turn it is drawn. Aliens may not use Item cards and will discard all Item cards after drawing them.

Adrenaline

Gives 1 stack of the Adrenaline Surge Status Effect.

Attack

Immediately attacks the sector you are currently in, killing any other player in that sector.

Cat

Gives 1 stack of the Feline Status Effect.

Clone*

Gives 1 stack of the Cloned Status Effect. This card now must be played before you are attacked. You will not have a chance to play this if another player attacks you.

Defense*

Gives 1 stack of the Armored Status Effect. This card now must be played before you are attacked. You will not have a chance to play this if another player attacks you.

Mutation

Immediately turns you into an Alien and switches your team from Human to Alien. You will remain in the same sector you were in at the time of playing the card.

Scanner

Upon playing this card, choose a Player. That Player's Team and Role is revealed to everyone in the game. (This card is not actually in the tabletop version, but I have added it as a way to implement the Medic's ability.)

Sedatives

Gives 1 stack of the Sedated Status Effect.

Sensor

Upon playing this card, choose a Player. That Player's exact position is revealed to everyone in the game.

Spotlight

Upon playing this card, choose a sector. Any Player in that sector or any adjacent sector immediately has their exact location revealed to everyone in the game.

Teleport*

Immediately moves you to a randomly selected Start Sector belonging to your team. For example, if you are a Human, it moves you to a Human Start Sector; If you are an Alien, it moves you to an Alien Start Sector. (If there is only 1 Start Sector belonging to your team, it will move you to that sector) Does not count as moving, so it can be played before or after moving, as desired.
