Noise Cards

Noise Cards are immediately discarded upon being drawn, causing the Player to make noise depending on the card

Red Card

Make noise in the sector you just moved into

Green Card

Make noise in any sector of your choosing

White Card

Make no noise

Item Cards

Item Cards are added to your hand upon being drawn. In addition to making no noise on this turn, you can also use the card at any point after drawing it, including on the turn you draw it
